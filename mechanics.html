Game Mechanics

Everything you need to know about how Echo of Abyss works.

Resources

Essence

  • Spent to summon minions
  • Refills to essence_max each turn
  • Starting max: 1
  • Grows by 1 when you choose “grow Essence” at end of turn
  • Hard cap: 10

Mana

  • Spent on spells, traps, runes, and environments
  • Refills to mana_max each turn
  • Starting max: 1
  • Grows by 1 when you choose “grow Mana” at end of turn

Combined Resource Cap

essence_max + mana_max ≤ 11

At the end of each turn, you choose to grow either Essence or Mana by 1 (not both). You cannot exceed a combined total of 11.


Conversion spells let you move resources between pools:

  • Energy Conversion (0 Mana): Convert up to 3 remaining Essence → Mana
  • Flux Siphon (0 Mana): Convert up to 3 remaining Mana → Essence

Card Types

Dual-Cost Minions

Some minions require both Essence and Mana (e.g., Runic Void Imp costs 2 Essence + 1 Mana). Both pools must have sufficient resources to play the card.

Board Layout

  • 5 minion slots per side (player and enemy)
  • Minions occupy one slot each; a full board cannot accept new minions
  • Slots retain their index for adjacency effects (e.g., Void Devourer sacrifices adjacent minions)
  • Max 3 trap/rune slots shared between traps and runes
  • 1 environment slot per side

Hand & Deck

  • Max hand size: 10 cards — cards drawn beyond 10 are burned/discarded
  • Opening hand: 3 cards
  • When deck is empty, discard pile reshuffles back in
  • Draw 1 card at the start of each turn

Combat Mechanics

Minion vs. Minion

  1. Both minions deal damage to each other simultaneously
  2. Shield absorbs first: shield_absorbed = min(damage, current_shield), then HP -= (damage - shield_absorbed)
  3. If HP ≤ 0: minion is destroyed, ON DEATH effects fire
  4. If attacker has Lifedrain: heals hero by attacker's effective ATK
  5. Attacker transitions to EXHAUSTED state

Minion vs. Hero

  1. Damage = attacker's effective ATK
  2. Hero HP reduced directly (heroes have no shield)
  3. If attacker has Lifedrain: heals hero by same amount

Guard Enforcement

If the opponent has ≥ 1 Guard minion, the attacker must target a Guard minion. Cannot target the hero or non-Guard minions while Guard exists.

Exception: Void Manifestation talent — Void Imp attacks become Void Bolts that ignore Guard entirely.

Effective ATK

effective_atk = current_atk + ATK_BONUS + TEMP_ATK - CORRUPTION_PENALTY

Minimum 0. A minion with 0 effective ATK cannot attack.

Turn Structure

Player Turn

  1. Turn Start Phase
    • Increment turn number
    • Refill Essence and Mana to their maximums
    • Draw 1 card (burn if hand is full)
    • Unexhaust all friendly minions (EXHAUSTED/SWIFT → NORMAL)
    • Expire temporary buffs
    • Regenerate shields (Shield Regen keywords)
    • Fire ON_PLAYER_TURN_START triggers (environment passives, rune auras, etc.)
  2. Action Phase — Play cards and attack with minions in any order. No limit on actions per turn.
  3. End Turn — Choose to grow Essence or Mana by 1 (if below combined cap of 11). Control passes to enemy.

Enemy Turn

  1. Turn Start — Enemy draws 2 cards, grows resources, refills, unexhausts, expires temp buffs, regenerates shields
  2. AI Play Phase — Enemy plays cards using encounter-specific AI profiles
  3. AI Attack Phase — Enemy minions attack with smart targeting
  4. End Enemy Turn — Control returns to player

Trap & Rune System

Trap Slots

Max 3 active traps/runes simultaneously (shared pool). Both traps and runes occupy slots from this pool.

Normal Traps

  • Placed face-down (hidden from opponent)
  • Trigger automatically when their event fires during enemy turn
  • Consumed (removed) on trigger by default
  • Destroyed by trap removal effects

Runes

  • Placed face-up (always visible)
  • Persistent passive aura — never auto-consumed
  • Count as ritual components
  • Destroyed by trap removal effects

Ritual System

A Ritual is a powerful one-time effect that fires automatically when specific rune conditions are met alongside an active Environment card.


Trigger Timing

  • When a rune is placed — checks if current runes satisfy any environment ritual
  • When an environment is placed — checks if runes already on board satisfy its rituals
  • Order of play does not matter

Resolution

  1. Check for valid 3-rune Grand Ritual first (requires Abyss Convergence talent)
  2. Check for valid 2-rune rituals defined by the active environment
  3. If valid: consume the required runes, then execute the ritual effect
  4. The environment remains active after the ritual fires

Void Marks & Void Bolt

Void Marks

  • Accumulated on the enemy hero during combat
  • Persist across turns within a single combat
  • Scale Void Bolt damage

Mark Sources

  • Piercing Void talent: +1 when Void Imp plays from hand
  • Void Bolt spell (with talent): +1
  • Void Imp Wizard: +1 on play
  • Mark the Target: +2 directly

Void Bolt Damage

total = base_damage + (void_marks × bonus_per_mark)


Base bonus per mark25
With Deepened Curse50 per mark

Any effect labeled “Void Bolt damage” scales with marks. Regular damage does not.


Void Manifestation: Void Imp attacks become Void Bolts — uses Void Bolt formula instead of physical ATK and ignores Guard.

Champion System

What are Champions?

Every encounter has a unique champion unit that is auto-summoned for free when a specific fight condition is met. Champions are not played from the enemy's deck — they appear automatically when the encounter's signature mechanic reaches a climax moment.


  • Cost: 0 Essence (free summon)
  • Max 1 per encounter — each fight has exactly one champion
  • Effects: Aura-type or keyword-based only (no on-play effects)
  • Death value: Killing a champion deals 20% of enemy hero max HP as direct damage to the enemy hero

Summon Conditions

Each champion has a unique auto-summon condition tied to the encounter's theme. A progress tracker shows how close the champion is to appearing:

Win & Loss Conditions

Victory

Enemy hero HP ≤ 0. Run advances; card/relic rewards offered. If final boss (Fight 15): permanent unlock cards offered.

Player hero HP ≤ 0. Run ends; return to main menu with option to retry.
