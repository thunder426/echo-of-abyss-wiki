Hero Passives

  • Void Imp Boost: Void Imp Clan minions have +1/+1 ATK and HP.
  • Void Imp Extra Copy: You may include up to 4 copies of Void Imp in your deck (instead of the normal 2).

Talent Trees

Talents are unlocked during a run using talent points. You start with 1 talent point. Each talent requires the previous tier in its branch.

Each branch unlocks a unique card pool when you pick its entry talent (Tier 0).

Branch 1: Endless Tide
Overwhelm the board with Void Imps. Unlocks the Endless Tide card pool.
Imp Evolution (Entry)
When you summon a Void Imp, add a Senior Void Imp to your hand. Trigger limit: once per turn.
Swarm Discipline
Void Imps gain +1 HP.
Imp Warband
When you summon a Senior Void Imp, all Void Imps gain +0.5 ATK permanently.
Void Echo (Capstone)
Whenever you draw a Void Imp, add a free copy to your hand.
Branch 2: Rune Master
Harness the Runes to unleash devastating grand rituals. Unlocks the Rune Master card pool.
Rune Caller (Entry)
Playing a Void Imp from hand draws a random Rune from your deck. That Rune costs 1 less Mana this turn.
Runic Attunement
All Rune aura effects are doubled (Void Rune: 2 dmg, Blood Rune: 2 heal, Dominion Rune: +2 ATK, Shadow Rune: 2 Corruption).
Ritual Surge
When any ritual fires, summon 2 Void Imps.
Abyss Convergence (Capstone)
Grand Ritual: Blood + Dominion + Shadow Runes on board simultaneously triggers Abyssal Dominion with no Environment required. Consumes all three Runes. Deals 3 damage to all enemy minions. All friendly Demons permanently gain +2/+2.
Branch 3: Void Resonance
Strike with amplified Void energy. Unlocks the Piercing Void card pool.
Piercing Void (Entry)
On play, Void Imps deal 2 Void Bolt damage and apply 1 Void Mark. Void Imps cost +1 Mana.
Deepened Curse
Void Mark bonus increases from +0.25 to +0.5 damage per stack.
Death Bolt
When a Void Imp dies, deal 1 Void Bolt damage to the enemy hero.
Void Manifestation (Capstone)
Void Imps' attacks become Void Bolts targeting the enemy hero, ignoring Guard entirely.

Talent Interaction Notes

  • Rune Caller fires only on Void Imps played from hand — not on tokens from Imp Evolution, Ritual Surge, or other sources.
  • Imp Evolution fires once per turn even if multiple Void Imps are played.
  • Runic Attunement doubles the value of each aura, not the number of times it fires.
  • Ritual Surge imps enter after ritual resolution — they benefit from permanent stat grants from the ritual.
  • Abyss Convergence Grand Ritual fires before any 2-rune environment ritual when all 3 runes are present.
  • Void Manifestation Imp attacks bypass Guard targeting entirely.
