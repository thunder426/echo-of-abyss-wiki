Passives

  • Feral Reinforcement: Imp summon mechanics.
  • Void Unraveling: On minion death, transfers Void Sparks to friendly board. On minion summon, may spawn Void Sparks.

Strategy

Watch out for Soul Collector — it instantly destroys Corrupted minions (and the enemy applies Corruption to your board). Keep your key minions free of Corruption stacks, or they'll be executed. Void Unraveling creates cascading Void Spark generation.

Act 3 The Void Rift

Fight 7: Rift Stalker
HP: 38
“The Void Rift World — a place where reality has frayed. Rift Stalkers phase between dimensions, attacking from angles that shouldn't exist.”

Key Cards

  • Phase Stalker (2E + 1 Spark): 4/3, Swift — fast, aggressive Spirit
  • Void Behemoth (3E + 2 Sparks): 4/6, Guard — massive wall
  • Void Bolt (3M): 5 Void Bolt damage to hero
  • Void Pulse (1M + 1 Spark): Draw 3 cards

Passives

  • Void Rift: Generates Void Sparks at the start of each enemy turn.
  • Void Empowerment: Buffs minion stats based on available Void Spark count.
Fight 8: Void Aberration
HP: 46
“A Void Aberration — a creature that should not exist in any plane. It was assembled from the broken remnants of things consumed by the rift.”

Key Cards

  • Dimensional Breach (1M + 2 Sparks): Summon 3 Void Sparks — creates Spark loops
  • Rift Collapse (2M + 1 Spark): 2 AoE damage to all enemy minions
  • Same Void Rift toolkit as Fight 7, but with more Spark generation

Passives

  • Void Rift: Turn start Void Spark generation.
  • Void Detonation: Void Sparks can detonate for damage when threshold is reached.
BOSS Fight 9: Void Herald
HP: 65
“The Void Herald speaks with the voice of the Abyss itself. It has crossed countless worlds before this one. It carries a message: the Abyss Sovereign is coming, and nothing will remain.”

Key Cards

  • Void Rift Lord (4E + 3 Sparks): 4/6, On play: set enemy Mana to 0 next turn — devastating mana denial
  • Full Void Rift toolkit: Phase Stalkers, Void Behemoths, Dimensional Breach, Rift Collapse

Passives

  • Void Rift: Turn start Void Spark generation.
  • Void Mastery: Halves all Void Spark costs — massive resource advantage. The Herald plays Spark cards at half price.

Strategy

Extremely high HP (65). Void Mastery halves all Spark costs, giving the Herald a massive tempo advantage. Void Rift Lord can lock down your Mana entirely. You need aggressive tempo and resource denial to keep up.

Act 4 — The Final Descent

Fight 10: Void Scout
HP: 50
“The Void Castle looms at the edge of existence. Void Scouts patrol its outer walls — swift, precise, and utterly loyal.”

Straightforward encounter with Abyssal Brutes (Guard) and Void Bolts (direct damage). No special passives.

Fight 11: Void Warband
HP: 56
“A full Void Warband stands between you and the castle's keep. These are the Sovereign's chosen soldiers — hardened by centuries of conquest across dying worlds.”

More Abyssal Brutes and direct damage. Scaled-up version of the Void Scout encounter.

Fight 12: Void Captain
HP: 62
“The Void Captain commands the castle's garrison. A veteran of a hundred conquests, she has never known defeat. She regards you with curiosity — a new species of prey.”

Further scaling with additional Abyssal Plague copies for AoE + Corruption pressure.

Fight 13: Void Ritualist Prime
HP: 70
“The Ritualist Prime is the Sovereign's high priest. He has spent his eternal life weaving void energy into a prison for the soul.”

Increased Void Bolt count (3 copies) for heavy direct-damage focus.

Fight 14: Void Champion
HP: 78
“The last guardian before the throne. The Void Champion was forged from pure abyss energy — no flesh, no weakness, no mercy.”

Maximum scaling before the final boss. 3 Brutes, 3 Void Bolts, 2 Plagues.

FINAL BOSS Fight 15: Abyss Sovereign
HP: 100
“At last. The Abyss Sovereign — the source of all corruption, the end of all things. It has devoured worlds without count. Today, it faces something it has never encountered: defiance.”

Enemy Deck

CardCopiesNotes
Abyssal Brute44E · 3/6 · Guard
Void Bolt33M · 5 Void Bolt to hero
Abyssal Plague22M · Corruption + 1 AoE

Strategy

The ultimate endurance test. 100 HP with no special passives — pure stat check. The Sovereign stalls behind Guards while burning you down with Void Bolts. Your deck needs to be highly refined by this point: strong board presence, efficient removal, and enough reach to punch through 100 HP.
