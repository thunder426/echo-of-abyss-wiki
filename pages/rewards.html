Rewards, Shop & Card Pools

How you earn cards, relics, and void shards throughout a run.

Card Pools

There is no permanent unlock mechanism for support pool cards. Your available card pools are determined entirely by your hero choice and talent selection during the current run.

Core Pools (Always Available)

Every hero has access to two core pools from the start:

  • Faction Core — Hero's faction cards (e.g., Abyss Order core for Lord Vael)
  • Neutral Core — Faction-neutral cards available to all heroes

Core pool cards can appear in normal fight rewards and shop.

Support Pools (Hero + Talent)

Determined by your hero and chosen talents:

  • Hero Common Pool — Always available for that hero (e.g., Vael Common for Lord Vael)
  • Talent Branch Pool — Unlocked when you pick the entry talent of a branch

Support pool cards appear in boss rewards and are guaranteed in shop.

Act Gating

Support pool cards are gated by act. A card's act gate determines when it becomes available as a reward or in the shop:

  • Act Gate 1: Available from the start of the run
  • Act Gate 2: Available after defeating the Act 1 boss (Fight 3)
  • Act Gate 3: Available after defeating the Act 2 boss (Fight 6)
  • Act Gate 4: Available after defeating the Act 3 boss (Fight 9)

Special Unlock

Nyx'ael, Void Sovereign

Unlock condition: Clear all 4 acts as Lord Vael (defeat the Abyss Sovereign in Fight 15).

Once unlocked, Nyx'ael can be included in your starting deck on future runs. This is the only card with a cross-run permanent unlock.

Fight Rewards

Normal Fight Reward

  • Win a normal fight → choose 1 card from 3 options
  • The 3 cards are randomly drafted from all available pools:
    • Faction core (Abyss Order)
    • Neutral core
    • Unlocked support pools (hero common + talent branch)
  • Deck limit: max 2 copies per card — if a drafted card would exceed this, it's discarded and another is drawn
  • Champions: max 1 copy
  • Also earns 1 Void Shard

Boss Fight Reward

  • Win a boss fight → choose 1 card from 3 options
  • The 3 cards are always drafted from support pools only (hero common + talent branch)
  • Same deck limit rules apply
  • Also earns 3 Void Shards
  • After card reward: relic selection (see below)
  • After relic: +1 talent point

Relic Rewards

After each boss fight, you choose a relic:

  • Act 1 Boss: Choose 1 of 2 relics from the Act 1 relic pool (4 options total, 2 offered)
  • Act 2+ Bosses: Choose 1 of 2 relics from that act's pool, or add +1 charge to a random existing relic you own

Relic options are unique per act — see the Relics page for the full list per act.

Void Shards

Void Shards are the in-run currency used to purchase cards and services at the shop.

SourceAmountWhen

Void Shard Economy per Run

Across a full 15-fight run:

  • Act 1: 2 normal fights (2 shards) + 1 boss (3 shards) = 5 shards
  • Act 2: 2 normal fights (2 shards) + 1 boss (3 shards) = 5 shards
  • Act 3: 2 normal fights (2 shards) + 1 boss (3 shards) = 5 shards
  • Act 4: 5 normal fights (5 shards) + 1 boss (3 shards) = 8 shards
  • Maximum total: 23 shards

Shop

The shop appears before each boss fight. It offers 4 cards and 2 services, all purchasable with Void Shards.

Card Slots (4 cards)

  • 2 slots guaranteed from support pool (active talent branch + hero common pool)
  • 2 slots from full pool (core sets + any unlocked support pools)
  • Standard cards cost 2 shards; Champion cards cost 4 shards
  • Champions are not offered in the first shop
  • Cards you already have 2 copies of (or 1 for Champions) are not offered

Service Slots (2 services)

2 random services are offered from the eligible pool. Selection is uniform random (no weighting). Some services are only available after the first shop.
