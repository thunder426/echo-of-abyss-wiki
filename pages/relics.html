Relics

Activated abilities with limited charges and cooldowns. Persist across a run; charges reset each combat.

Relic Rules

Activation

  • 1 relic activation per turn — cannot fire multiple relics in the same turn
  • Cooldown = turns before available (first available turn = cooldown + 1)
  • After each use, the relic goes on cooldown again for the same duration

Acquisition

  • Act 1 boss: Choose 1 of 2 random relics from the Act 1 pool
  • Act 2+ bosses: Choose 1 of 2 random relics from that act's pool, or +1 charge to a random existing relic
  • Charges reset at the start of each new combat

Act 1 Utility & Resource

Scout's Lantern
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 3 turns
Draw 2 cards.
Imp Talisman
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 3 turns
Add a Void Imp to your hand.
Mana Shard
Charges: 2 · Cooldown: 2 turns
Gain +2 Mana this turn (cannot exceed max).
Bone Shield
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 5 turns
Your hero takes no damage until your next turn.

Act 2 Tempo & Removal

Void Lens
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 4 turns
Cast Abyssal Plague for free: apply 1 Corruption to all enemy minions and deal 100 damage to all enemies.
Soul Anchor
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 4 turns
Summon a 300/300 Void Spark and grant it Guard.
Dark Mirror
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 3 turns
Reduce the cost of your next card by 2 Essence and 2 Mana (minimum 0).
Blood Chalice
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 3 turns
Deal 500 damage to a target enemy.

Act 3 Powerful & Game-Swinging

Void Hourglass
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 5 turns
Take an extra turn after this one.
Oblivion Seal
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 5 turns
Summon a 500/500 Void Demon with Lifedrain.
Nether Crown
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 4 turns
All friendly minions gain +200 ATK this turn.
Phantom Deck
Charges: 1 · Cooldown: 4 turns
Add a copy of your 3 highest-cost cards to your hand.
